WAYS TO GIVE

Multiple channels, shared mission

Financial Donations

Power our weekly programs through Paystack or bank transfer. Funds cover sanitary kits, transport, and library resources.

Equipment & Supplies

Donate dance outfits, speakers, books, stationery, or waste collection gear. Every item finds immediate use.

Skills & Mentorship

Coach our dancers, host workshops, or mentor youth in leadership, entrepreneurship, and technology.

Corporate & Group Partners

Co-brand clean-up drives, sponsor library expansions, or match employee donations.
