Who we are

The heartbeat of Kibera’s youth movement

Huit Fambo Foundation is a youth-led collective transforming lives through dance, mentorship, environmental action, and safe spaces. We organize, advocate, and celebrate the brilliance of young people in Kibera.

Partner With Us

OUR STORY

Born in the heart of Kibera, Huit Fambo began as a dance crew determined to bring joy to streets facing inequality. Today, we are a foundation mobilizing youth power for environmental justice, education, and safe spaces.

We are relentless about creating opportunities for young people to thrive. From dance floors to classrooms, waste collection routes to community libraries, we use every platform available to advocate for dignity and possibility.

Our work is shaped by lived experience. We understand the urgency of reforming systems that fail young people; we also know the strength that emerges when we invest in their creativity, leadership, and wellbeing.

MISSION & VISION

Mission

To empower and reform the youth by providing safe, nurturing, and creative environments where they can learn, lead, and champion social change.

Vision

A resilient Kibera where young people drive sustainable development through dance, advocacy, mentorship, and environmental stewardship.

OUR PILLARS

Where impact comes to life

Dance for Change

Performances and creative showcases that raise awareness about gender equality, environmental justice, and youth empowerment.

Environmental Action

Weekly waste collection across 40 households, plastic sorting, and recycling initiatives that keep our neighborhood clean.

Mentorship & Life Skills

School and community sessions covering personal development, menstrual hygiene, and responsible decision-making.

Community Library & Safe Space

In partnership with Masaku Lions Club, we offer a learning hub serving 35–40 children every week.

ACHIEVEMENTS & RECOGNITION

What we've achieved so far

  • Community Reach: Reached over 1,400 residents through waste collection and outreach programs.
  • Library Impact: Running a community library serving 35–40 children daily.
  • Education Sessions: Delivered 100+ mentorship and life-skills sessions in local schools.
  • Environmental Action: Organized monthly cleanups and established a plastic sorting pilot.
  • Performance & Awareness: Hosted Dance for Change showcases raising awareness on social issues.
  • Recognition: Acknowledged by local community groups and partnered organizations for youth leadership.

HOW WE WORK

Grassroots + Creative + Consistent

Listen & Co-create

We begin every program with conversations in households, schools, and youth hangouts to understand urgent needs.

Prototype & Perform

We test ideas quickly—through dance pieces, clean-up pilots, and mentorship circles—and refine based on feedback.

Mentor & Mobilize

Young leaders receive mentorship and take ownership of projects, ensuring continuity beyond any single event.

Measure & Celebrate

We document stories, track numbers, and celebrate wins publicly to keep motivation and accountability high.
