Behind the scenes

How a typical Saturday unfolds

06:00 am — Call time & debriefs

Arrival for the team, talk about the day ahead, Role distribution.

07:00 am — Community activations, distribution and sorting

Dance pop-ups energize the neighborhood while cleanup crews collect and sort waste. Separating organic from inorganic waste then disposing them in a designated area.

08:00 am — Washing tools and returning tools to stores

Washing the used tools e.g. brooms, rakes, and returning them to their respective stores.

09:00 am — Debrief and Library Opening

Teams log impact numbers, share stories, and plan next week’s priorities as we open the community library.
