Program approach

Mentorship Circles

Circles meet weekly and follow modular lesson plans: wellbeing, career pathways, menstrual health, conflict resolution, and community leadership. Sessions are interactive, mixing role-play, group reflection, and action planning.

What mentors do

  • Facilitate safe discussion
  • Coach small group activities
  • Connect youth to opportunities and referrals

Session format

Each circle runs for 8–12 weeks with clear topics, reflection questions, and actionable commitments. Mentors log attendance and follow up on referrals.
