What it is

Dance for Change

Dance for Change blends choreography, spoken word, and community theatre. Each piece is co-created with local youth and tailored for the audience—whether it's a school, market square, or a public forum. Beyond performance we host post-show discussions and action moments where audiences commit to concrete steps like joining a cleanup or supporting a mentorship circle.

  • Short narrative dances that tackle a single issue (10–20 minutes)
  • Interactive debriefs led by youth facilitators
  • Practical entry points for volunteers and partners
